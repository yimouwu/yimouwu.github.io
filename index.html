Hello! I'm a M.Phil. student at CUHK, advised by Prof. Zheng Li. I am drawn to philosophy and machines, to how robots come to perceive, understand, and interact with the physical world. My research currently focuses on SLAM, embodied AI, and 3D scene understanding.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. from CUHK Shenzhen, where I worked with Prof. Huihuan Qian, Haizhou Li, and Pinjia He. I was also a Visiting Student at UC Berkeley.
