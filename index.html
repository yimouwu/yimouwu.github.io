Biography

I'm a 4th-year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ), currently advised by Prof. Huihuan Qian and Postdoc Ruoyu Xu. My research interest lies in Robotics, Embodied AI and Machine Learning. I aim to develop advanced control algorithms and intelligent systems for robotic applications.

I have also worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant under Prof. Haizhou Li, where I focused on signal processing, data analysis, and Brain-Computer Interfaces using ECoG signals. Additionally, under Prof. Pinjia He, I worked on system optimization by developing a load generator to stress-test and improve the resilience of microservices.

I am currently applying for Ph.D. and Master's programs starting in Fall 2025. I would be grateful for any opportunity to discuss potential collaborations or receive your guidance. Please feel free to contact me by email if you have any suggestions or advice.
