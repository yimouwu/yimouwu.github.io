Yimou (Kevin) Wu

M.Phil. @ CUHK

I'm a M.Phil. student at CUHK, advised by Prof. Zheng Li. I work on 3D scene reconstruction, dynamic scene understanding, and visual perception systems that operate in challenging real-world environments.

My research goal is to build machines that understand space, motion, and structure from vision alone — robustly, in the wild.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. from [Undergraduate University]. Outside research, I think about philosophy of mind, the mathematics of perception, and the long arc of how intelligence develops.
